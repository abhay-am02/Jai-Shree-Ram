About Lord Ram

Lord Ram, the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu, is a symbol of virtue, morality, and dharma. His life and journey, described in the ancient Indian epic Ramayana, inspire millions to walk the path of truth, devotion, and duty.

The chant "Jay Shri Ram" (Victory to Lord Ram) is often used to express devotion, seek blessings, and inspire courage. It unites people across India in festivals, prayers, and spiritual gatherings.
